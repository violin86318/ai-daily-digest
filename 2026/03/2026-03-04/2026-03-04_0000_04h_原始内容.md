Apple introduces the new MacBook Air with M5

CUPERTINO, CALIFORNIA — Apple today announced the new MacBook Air with M5, bringing exceptional performance and expanded AI capabilities to the world's most popular laptop. M5 features a faster CPU and next-generation GPU with a Neural Accelerator in each core, enabling MacBook Air to power through a variety of workflows, from creative projects to complex AI tasks.

**Key Features:**
- M5 chip: 10-core CPU + 10-core GPU, Neural Accelerator in each core
- AI performance up to 4x faster than M4, 9.5x faster than M1
- Starting storage doubled to 512GB, configurable up to 4TB
- Wi-Fi 7 and Bluetooth 6 (first time on MacBook Air)
- Up to 18 hours battery life
- 13-inch and 15-inch models available
- Colors: sky blue, midnight, starlight, silver
- Price: $1,099 (13-inch), $1,299 (15-inch)
- Available for pre-order starting March 4, shipping March 11

## 文章 2: 印度最高法院震怒：法官引用 AI 伪造判例

**来源**: 印度快报 (Indian Express)
**链接**: https://indianexpress.com/article/legal-news/ai-hallucination-again-in-a-court-order-sc-talks-of-institutional-concern-10561833/
**发布时间**: 2026-03-03

---

**事件概述:**
- 安得拉邦一名初级法官在财产纠纷案中引用了4个 AI 生成的伪造判例
- 案件涉及争议财产，法官依据这些伪造判例做出了裁决
- 最高法院称这是"机构性关切"问题，而非简单的"决策错误"
- 最高法院已暂停原判决，并向总检察长、副总检察长和印度律师协会发出通知

**法院态度:**
- 最高法院明确区分了"使用 AI 的善意错误"和"不当行为"
- 将引用伪造判例定性为"不当行为"（misconduct），而非简单错误
- 这一定性显著升级了后果的严重性

**背景:**
- 去年以来，全球多国法院陆续出现 AI 辅助导致的错误判决
- 美国、英国也有类似案例
- 2026年2月13日，印度最高法院发现请愿人引用了不存在的判决
