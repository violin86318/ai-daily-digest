## Cursor AI 融资新闻
- 来源：CNBC
- URL: https://www.cnbc.com/2026/04/19/cursor-ai-2-billion-funding-round.html

An artificial intelligence startup Cursor is in talks to raise a $2 billion fundraising round at an over $50 billion valuation. Andreessen Horowitz is slated to co-lead the new investment round, with Nvidia and Thrive Capital also expected to participate.

Cursor announced in November that it closed a $2.3 billion funding round at a $29.3 billion post-money valuation.
